Who we are

We are Christians at McMaster university, and we want to hear from strangers what they believe in. We want to create a safe space for healthy conversations about Christianity and other beliefs.

We know there is a stigma about judgmental Christians, which can be true! We know that church has been a place of hurt for some, and Christians haven't always been the most welcoming.

We want to fix that! Come sit down with us, and have some Good Talks :)

Is this for me?

  1. I know little about Christianity, but I'm open to talk
  2. I have questions about Christianity that I want to process
  3. I have had negative experiences with Christian institutions or individual Christians
  4. I enjoy having interesting conversations
