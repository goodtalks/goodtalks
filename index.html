A Letter to Our Friends

Hi,

When was the last time you had a conversation with a complete stranger?
Not your Uber driver. Not your local narcotics dispenser. A complete stranger!

These days, it's so easy to be stuck with the same friends with the same views.
Especially when it comes to personal beliefs or religion, we tend to shy away from talking about it with new people. Why is that?

We call ourselves "Good Talks".
We self-identify as non-bigoted, open-minded Christians that want to hear from strangers on what they believe in. Trust us, we don't have an agenda. All we want to do is listen to what you have to say, have real talks, and lower your GPA. You're welcome.

Sincerely,
Stephanie, Jonathan, Seojin
